## Compliance guardrails
- Frame wipes as **plaque prevention between professional cleanings** — never as treating or reversing dental disease.
- Systemic-health link: always "associated with," never causal.
- Do not claim VOHC acceptance unless Roofus's own product is on the VOHC accepted list.
- "80% by age 3" (the viral version) traces to secondary sources; the defensible wording is "over age 3."
