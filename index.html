The Proof

Periodontal disease is the most common condition affecting adult dogs.

That's the American Veterinary Dental College's wording, and studies cited by Cornell's College of Veterinary Medicine put it at 80–90% of dogs over age 3. It's also largely preventable.

Plaque forms within hours of a meal and begins hardening into tartar within 24 hours. Once it hardens, no chew, additive, or wipe removes it. Only professional scaling under anesthesia does. The entire game is removing plaque while it's still soft.

The AVMA calls regular brushing "the single most effective thing" for at-home dental health between cleanings, and VCA Animal Hospitals lists daily brushing or wiping among the most effective ways to remove plaque before it turns to tartar.

Frequently Asked Questions

 
Do wipes replace toothbrushing?
Mechanical plaque removal is the goal, and Buffbead™ delivers it in a form most dogs accept. If your dog tolerates a toothbrush, brush. If your dog is like most dogs, the wipe is the routine that will actually happen, and consistency is what matters.
Will my dog actually let me do this?
Most dogs accept a finger far more readily than a brush. Your hand is something they already trust, and the peanut butter scent does the rest. Start with a few seconds of lip-lifting, reward, build up. Most parents report a full two-side wipe within a week.
How often should I use them?
Daily for best results. Plaque forms within hours of a meal and starts hardening into tartar within 24 hours (VCA Animal Hospitals), so a daily 30-second wipe beats a weekly deep session. Once breath is under control, many parents maintain at 2 to 3 times a week. Each canister holds 60 dual-sided wipes.
Can wipes remove existing tartar?
No, and no home product honestly can. Hardened tartar needs a professional cleaning. Wipes remove the soft plaque stage before it mineralizes, which is how you slow new tartar and protect the investment after a professional cleaning.
Are they safe for puppies and seniors?
Yes. Plant-based formula with no alcohol, no xylitol, no parabens. Safe to lick, safe to swallow. The unscented version suits the most sensitive mouths, and starting young is the best predictor of lifelong acceptance.
Is this really one-time? No subscription?
Correct. Every kit is a one-time purchase. No auto-ship, no hidden subscription, no card on file. When you run out, come back for the deal, or stock up with the Full Family Plan and don't think about it for six months.
How does the 60-day guarantee work?
Use the wipes for two months. If your dog's breath isn't noticeably fresher and their teeth aren't visibly cleaner, email us and we refund every cent. No return shipping, no forms.
Can I use them on my cat?
The formula is designed for dogs. Cats have different sensitivities, and much stronger opinions about fingers in their mouths. Check with your vet before using any dog dental product on a cat.
When should I see the vet instead?
Loose teeth, bleeding gums, heavy brown tartar, dropping food, pawing at the mouth: book a veterinary exam first. Wipes are daily prevention, not treatment for existing dental disease.
